Modern Living Room Renovation

New York City, USA

❮ ❯

Project Overview

This modern living room renovation project focused on blending functionality with a warm, inviting aesthetic. Key elements include open space planning, natural light maximization, and carefully curated furniture and décor. Sustainable materials were chosen to reflect both style and environmental consciousness.

Design Details

The color palette incorporates soft neutrals with accents of gold and wood tones, creating a sophisticated yet cozy ambiance. Lighting fixtures were strategically placed to enhance architectural features and highlight art pieces. This project showcases a perfect balance between contemporary design and comfort.
